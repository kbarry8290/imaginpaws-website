Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 1st, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how ImaginPaws ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect pet photos that you upload to our app for AI processing. We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your pet photos solely to provide AI transformation services. Your photos are processed using our AI tools to create the requested transformations.

3. Data Storage

Your pet photos are stored securely on our servers to provide you access to your transformations. We do not share your photos with third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your photos from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Apple's App Store for app distribution and payment processing. Please review Apple's privacy policy for information about how they handle your data.

6. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your photos and account data at any time by contacting us.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@imaginpaws.com
